## About Me

I'm a 1st year MSc student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto. I am supervised by [David Lindell](https://davidlindell.com) at the [Toronto Computational Imaging Group](https://compimaging.dgp.toronto.edu). My research interests span computer vision, generative AI, neural representations, and computational imaging. 

I completed my undergraduate degree in [Engineering Science](https://engsci.utoronto.ca/program/what-is-engsci/) at the University of Toronto, where I pursued a major in [Machine Intelligence](https://engsci.utoronto.ca/program/majors/machine-intelligence/) and minor in [Robotics](https://undergrad.engineering.utoronto.ca/academics-registration/minors-certificates/undergraduate-engineering-minors/robotics-and-mechatronics-minor/). I was supervised by [Florian Shkurti](http://www.cs.toronto.edu/~florian/) for my undergraduate thesis on multi-agent trajectory prediction and uncertainty calibration in autonomous driving.
